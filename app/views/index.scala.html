Highfield Cricket Club

A BRIEF OVERVIEW - We are Southampton based and have 2 teams in the Hampshire Cricket League on Saturdays. Our First XI gained promotion as County 3 South league champions to County 2 in 2013 and our Second XI currently play in Regional Division 2 South West. Both teams play their matches at Hardmoor Sports Ground, voted HCL Ground of the Year 2008 along with the top ground marks from opposition teams in for 2009, 2010, 2011 & 2012. We play Evening League cricket throughout the summer in the Taurus Hampshire Border League and during the winter months we play in 2 Indoor Leagues, The SECL Indoor League at the Rose Bowl and the South Hants Indoor League at Fleming Park. We also have regular winter net sessions.

We are always looking for new fun luvin' players and if you are looking for a new team to join, or just want to enjoy your cricket please contact us.
